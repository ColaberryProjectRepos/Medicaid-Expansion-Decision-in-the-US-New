Medicaid Expansion Decision in the US
1. Set the dashboard size to Custom 1150x980
2. Select show dashboard title the title box size is x=8, y=8, w=1134, h=41
3. Select tiled under object and drag horizontal container into the viz Under the title box.
4. Drag image box onto the container, the edit image box pops up, check fit image then choose the watermark image. The watermark image size is w=2016, h=1134.
5.  Select floating and drag image box unto the viz, choose fit image from the pop-up edit image box, load the Colaberry logo size w=209x h=59. Resize the image box, size x=8, y=6, w=222 and h=37.
6. Hold down the shift key and drag, horizontal container into the viz on top of the background container. Resize the container x=8, y=53,w=1132,h=39, select the second solid line as border and color grey #b4b4b4 then color the background #2d2463 (Blue).
7. Drag Health Report vs YOY KPI unto the viz, push to the edge of the blue line, resize x=8, y=53,w=222 h=299.Border color=#b4b4b4  with thick line 2, background is set to none. Double click on the title and color white with font size 12, then center.
8. Drag horizonal container under Health Report vs YOY KPI while holding down the shift key and resize x=8, y=352,w=1132,h=39, color the background blue #2d2463.
9. Drag Underlying Cause KPI into the viz under the Health Report vs YOY KPI, on top of the colored blue box. Resize and position. X=8 , y=350  , w=222  , h =159. Set the border to the second thick line and color #b4b4b4.  Double click on the title to color white and center. Set background to none.
10. Drag Health Report vs YoY graph onto the canvas. Resize x=230, y=53,w=576 h=299. Border color=#b4b4b4  with second thick line , background is set to none. Double click on the title and color the title white with font size 12 and center. Position the two legend, the left one size x=295,y=113,w=98, y=40 and the right one size is x=593, y=112, w=206, h=41. Click on the legend and on the small down pointing arrow then Uncheck the title.
11. Drag Medicaid Map next to Health Report vs YoY. Resize to x=806, y=53,  w=335,   h=299. Legend size is x=887, y=325,w=246,h=19.Set the background to none. Then Double click on the title and color the title white with font size 12 and center.
12. Drag horizonal container under Underlying Cause KPI while holding down the shift key and resize x=8, y=509,w=222,h=39, color the background blue #2d2463.
13. Drag Uninsured Population KPI into the viz on top of the blue container under the Underlying Cause KPI. Resize x=8, y=508, w=222 , h=177. Set the background to none. Double click on the title and color the title white with font size 12 and then center
14. Drag Underlying Cause of Death graph unto the canvas. Resize x=330, y=352,w=455 h=294. Border color=#b4b4b4 with second thick line, background is set to none. Double click on the title and color the title white with font size 12 and center. Position the two legend, the left one size x=232,y=648,w=140, y=40 and the right one size is x=455, y=648, w=219, h=44. Click on the legend and on the small down pointing arrow then Uncheck the title.
15. Drag Yearly Uninsured Trend graph next to Underlying Cause of Death graph unto the canvas. Resize x=685, y=352,w=456 h=294. Border color=#b4b4b4 with with second thick line, background is set to none. Double click on the title and color the title white with font size 12 and middle position. Position the two legend, the left one size x=734,y=647,w=140, y=40 and the right one size is x=949, y=647, w=176, h=40. Click on the legend and on the small down pointing arrow then Uncheck title.
16. Click on the down arrow on the Yearly Uninsured Trend graph and select Filters, Year of Year. Resize the box x=857, y= 11, w=253, h=36.
