Map 21

                   

1. Under Medicaid Expansion Status, double click on Medicaid State
2. Under Marks select Map and then center the map to show mainland United States.
3. Drag Medicaid State unto Label on the Marks Card
4. Drag the State Medicaid Status into color on the Marks card.
5. You can edit State Medicaid Status color to change by clicking on the down arow and double clicking Adopted/ Not Adopted and changing the colors as follows: Adopted - #00aaff  Not Adopted - #2d2463    Click OK.
6. Select Map from the top menu, Map Options and uncheck all the boxes.
