Renaming the Tables 12

                                                             


1. Right Click on the table name to rename title.
2. Change the title “Health Report” to Health Status Report
3. Under Health Report Dimension:
“Health Report (Health Report)” to HSR Category
‘’Year’’ HSR Year
4. Under Health Report Measure:
Rename “Value” to HSR Value

5. Under Medicaid Expansion Status Dimension:
Rename “Location” to Medicaid State

6. Under Underlying Cause of Death, 2015
Change the title “Underlying Cause of Death, 2015” to Underlying Cause of Death
Under Dimension
“State” to UC State
“Year” to UC Year
Under Measure
“Crude Rate” UC Crude Rate 
“Death” to UC Deaths
“Population” to UC Population

7. Under Uninsured 
Change “Uninsured Full Data” to Uninsured Data
under Dimension
“Location” Uninsured State
“Year” Uninsured Year
